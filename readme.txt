Quickly insert a header for you post in seconds! No need to design anything from scratch.

== Description ==

Just select an image from your local files or enter an online URL to an image file that you feel is related to your post, add a related title, and hit upload, thats it! This plugin will automatically resize the image, insert your title and merge the template design in a sec, making your post look more professional and giving your visitors some imagery. Please let me know of any improvements you would like for the next version, and if you like it please rate it. You can contact me [here](http://www.shannonreca.com).

== Installation ==

1. Upload `plugin-name.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I use Post Quick Header? =

When you're in post editor you should see the "Post Quick Header" icon towards the right of your editor's icons. Click on it, and fill out the form requirements. Finally, just "Insert Header."
